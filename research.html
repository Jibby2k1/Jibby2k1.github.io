Research

Projects & programs

My work sits in two umbrellas: (1) research in the University of Florida’s Computational NeuroEngineering Lab (CNEL) — a group that studies how to measure and model brain and biological signals — and (2) community projects through the IEEE Signal Processing Society (SPS) student chapter at UF. I’m advised by Prof. José C. Principe and also collaborate with Prof. Joel Harley’s SmartDATA Lab.
